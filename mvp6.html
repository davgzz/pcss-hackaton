Stephen curry (2016)

Why He Won MVP

Stephen Curry deserved the MVP due to his exceptional leadership and unmatched scoring ability. He revolutionized the game with his three-point shooting, breaking records and consistently delivering clutch performances. Curry's ability to elevate his team, leading the Warriors to one of the best records in the league, showcased his invaluable impact on both ends of the court. His impressive consistency and dominance throughout the season made him a deserving MVP.
